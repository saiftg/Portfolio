About Me

Hello, my name is Saif Mahmud. I am full-stack developer, originally from New York city, currently based in Atlanta, GA. I was born in Saudi Arabia to Bangladeshi parents, where I lived for 16 years before moving to the US. I have a Bsc in Business Management from SUNY Plattsburgh and for a while I was working in a related field in New York. However, in the last few years I've been getting increasingly interested in programming languages. I wanted to know how and what makes websites and apps tick. After discussing with my wife about the pros and cons of a career change, I decided to take the plunge and join a coding bootcamp. I had always wanted to eventually move down south, so I looked for bootcamps in Atlanta, an up and coming tech city. So here I am now after completing a 16 week fullstack course from DigitalCrafts, ready to begin a new chapter in my life.
